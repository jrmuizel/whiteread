Fast version of std::str::SplitWhitespace, but with some drawbacks.

It considers to be whitespace everything with codepoint <= 0x20 (this includes " \t\n\r", but also some other unprintable characters). It doesn't consider to be whitespace any of non-ascii UTF whitespace characters (such as non-breaking space).
